Dresses and Men’s Suits for Weddings, Proms & Special Occasions in 2026 Styles


Attending formal events are usually celebration of a significant event that calls for the right attire. Here at JJ's House, we have a range of dresses at amazing prices that is perfect for formal events of any kind. Whether it's a wedding ceremony, prom party, or any special occasion, you can definitely find a dress to match the event theme. Start shopping for your next formal event and if you need any help, our team is always ready to assist you in picking the most suitable dress (that fits right!) for your event.


Wedding Dresses

JJ's House offers a wide selection of gorgeous wedding dresses in different silhouettes, fabrics and color combinations to match any wedding theme. Be it a black-tie dinner banquet or a beach wedding ceremony, there will be a suitable dress that not only matches the venue and theme but also everyone's budget with our affordably priced dresses.


Bridesmaid Dresses

The closest friends in your life deserve a dress that not only fits perfectly but also complements your wedding theme. With a range of colors, silhouettes and styles to pick from, you can find affordable dresses that not only matches the wedding theme, but also caters to everyone's budget.


Mother of the Bride and Groom Dresses

Weddings are a significant milestone not only for the couple but also for the mothers of the bride and groom. Our stunning and elegant dresses ensure they look their absolute best while standing proud on this special day.


Junior Bridesmaid Dresses

Our junior bridesmaid dresses are not only affordable but also adorable. With different styles and colors in a range of sizes to choose from, there will be a dress that suits young bridesmaid of any age.


Flower Girl Dresses

Flower girls set the tone for the ceremony, and their dresses should be just as special. Our collection features adorable flower girl dresses in white, ivory, and champagne, with additional colors available for a personalized touch.


Wedding Guest Dresses

We offer a curated collection of wedding guest dresses to ensure you are well-dressed for the wedding ceremony. From floor-length gowns, to mini and maxi dresses, there will be a dress that cater to the specific wedding venue and theme. Winter or summer weddings? Not a problem, JJ's House got you covered for the weather as well with our offerings of dresses in different fabrics.


Prom Dresses

Our stunning collection of prom dresses are not only affordable but also versatile to match most prom themes. Whether it's a glamourous ballroom event or a party at the school hall, there will be a dress that gets you all set for the party!


Cocktail Dresses

Our cocktail and party dress collection are a mix between playful and elegance. Whether you prefer to steal the spotlight at black-tie affairs or dolling up for an after-party celebration, there will be a dress that make you stand out at any event.


Men's Suits

Our collection of men's suits comes in a range of silhouettes from classic and slim to modern fits, catering to all kinds of formal events such as weddings, proms, and special celebrations. With thoughtfully designed styles and comfortable fabrics, you’ll look sharp, confident, and fully event-ready.


Accessories

We offer a variety of accessories to complete your look for any special occasion. You can find every accessory you need for any event, from hair pins to footwear and bags that will elevate your overall outfit by adding that finishing touch to it.
